A new economic corridor

Connection between the Baltic States and the biggest economic centers.

Connection between Nord, East and Central Europe contributes to:

  • increasing of new cargo flows
  • development of connected industries&infrastructure
  • development of logistics services
  • tourism
  • new working place
  • favorably influence prices of goods sold in partner countries
  • reduction of traffic congestion(from road to rail)

Political point

The intention of the Rail Baltic project is to fully integrate Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania in the whole European transportnetwork.

  • Integration of new EU countries
  • Cooperation of partner countries of EU on the international level

Benefits

  • Travel safety
  • Shorter travel time
  • Productive travel time
  • Cost savings
  • Environmentally friendly way of travelling

Criticism

  • Questions about cost effectiveness in the nearest future
  • Building new railway instead of upgrading the existing network
  • The differents between gauge in partner countries
  • Argues between the countries about the route(biggest cities such Vilinius, Tartu are not included)
